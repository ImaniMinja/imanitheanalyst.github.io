World Population Pivot Tables

Pivot Tables and Pivot Charts are one of my favorite features in Excel, as a close follow-up feature, pivot tables. Pivot charts visualize the data expressed in a pivot table, giving us insight at a glance. Pivot tables provide a simple approach to reformatting columns and rows, transforming them into groupings, statistics, or summaries.

Main Objective

The main objective of this project was to Understand the Dynamics, Continental Variance and Impacts of Events

Exploratory Analysis

The analysis was conducted based on key questions such as:

  • What level of detail is required?
  • What specific world population metrics and key indicators are essential to include in the Excel presentation?
  • Are there any external factors, such as global events or migration patterns, that may impact world population trends and should be addressed in the presentation?
  • What are the primary goals and objectives of the world population Excel presentation, and how will its success be measured or evaluated?
  • Where will the data for world population come from, and how will data accuracy and reliability be ensured in the Excel presentation?

    • Notable Findings

    I discovered trends in population growth and decline. and Identify regions experiencing demographic shifts.
